About the Game

My Virtual Item Requisition Upgrade Simulator is an idle clicking game for your desktop featuring the freshest and dankest memes.

How to play the game

Click the work button to gain coins! You can open boxes to find items that will boost the power of your clicks or give you passive income. There are over 20 items with different powers! Collect them all!

There are three tiers of boxes! The more expensive the box, the more cool prizes you can win!

Normal Box (Cost: 50 coins): Normal boxes have small prizes to help you start out your Item Requisition Upgrade journey!

Ultra Boxes (Cost: 500 coins): Ultra boxes contain upgraded prizes to propel you to the next level of of Item Requisition!

Prestige Boxes (Cost: 500 coins): Prestige Boxes are the best boxes you can find all games giving you some of the coolest items, most coins, and biggest buffs to the game!

Leave your game on overnight to build up the maximum amount of coins and fulfill your loot box opening urges!

Coin Balance: This is your current balance! You need to have enough coins in order to purchase the boxes!

Coins Per Second: This is the number of coins you gain passively. If you leave this program on, the coins will accumulate while you are AFK!

Coins Per Click: This is the number of coins you gain per click! You start out the lowest of the lows and earn a measly wage of 1 coin per work.
